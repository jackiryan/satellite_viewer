Hi! I’m Jacqueline Ryan, a Data Visualization Developer at NASA’s Jet Propulsion Laboratory in Los Angeles, California. My work blends software engineering, data science, and spacecraft operations to create interactive visualizations of science data. Over the years, I’ve worked on a number of NASA missions, including the Mars Perseverance and Curiosity Rovers, Cassini Orbiter, and various Earth-observing spectroscopy experiments.
